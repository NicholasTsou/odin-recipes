Read Bean Paste Buns

Ingredients:

  • 2 2/5 cup of all purpose flour
  • 40g Sugar
  • 180 ml warm milk OR 150 ml or warm water
  • 1 TBS vegetable oil
  • pinch of salt
  • 200g red bean paste filling

Steps for making this delicious snack:

  1. Get a pot and put it on low, low heat
  2. Add milk and use a thermometer to heat until 35C
  3. Add to a large bowl with the flour, sugar, yeast, vegetagble oil and salt
  4. Mix with a spoon or mixer with paddles for kneading/after switch to your hands to knead the dough
  5. Get everything worked in nice and good before setting it in a room temperature area for an hour for the
    dough to rise. What we did was we set the oven to 250 F and put a dish towel over the bowl. We set this on top
    of the oven to get the heat.
  6. When there's about 20 minutes left, add water to the steamer and set it to steam.
  7. After an hour, add like a tablespoon or 2 more of the flour and knead some more
  8. When it seems like it's not too rough, break up into about 6 little dough balls
  9. Use your fingers to flatten these out but make the edges thinner than the center.
    I started from the center and pushed/pinched outward until it seemed wide enough, that
    it could fold into a ball again
  10. Spoon the red bean paste into the center and close it up nice and tight.
  11. Flip these over and shape into a dome as much as you can
  12. Line the steamer bowl with parchment paper
  13. So here, we were supposed to place them, have the steamer off and let them prove for another 20 minutes.

  14. When they are done steaming, turn off the steamer and leave them in there for about 5 more minutes
